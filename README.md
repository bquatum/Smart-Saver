SMART SAVER APP

---------------------What the Product Does----------------------

The Smart saver App is designed to connect with prepaid meter systems, enabling users to monitor, plan and manage their power usage and expenses. This app transforms into a financial tool, helping users avoid unexpected outages by providing insights, reminders and automation.

----------Why It Matters---------------

Most people only realize their electricity units are finished when the lights go off, often at the most inconvenient times.  That’s a real problem many homes and offices face daily.

Smart Saver solves this issue by:

	•	Providing real time visibility into your power usage.
	•	Assisting users in budgeting and forecasting power consumption.
	•	Reducing energy waste and unnecessary expenses.
	•	Enabling seamless recharges through mobile payments.

It’s not just about managing power but it's about financial awareness, control and convenience.

 -------------Who It’s For-----------
 
	•	Homeowners and tenants are tired of sudden blackouts.
	•	Small business and office owners managing shared power use.
	•	Estate and facility managers tracking multiple meters.

-----------Key Features & Technologies:------------------------------------------------

Real Time Update: The app will monitor and track the power unit usage and spending.

Auto Topup: The app will be connected to a mobile bank app, wallet and card for seamless recharge.

Budget & Forecast: The app will predict when the power unit will finish and set usage target for users

Smart Alerts: The app will send notification to users when usage spikes or power unit balance is low.

Power Usage History & Tips: The app will provide tips on how to reduce and manage power usage and visual graph on power usage and payment history.

--------------------------------------------------Technologies (Planned Stack):-----------------------------------------------------------------

Frontend:  Flutter (cross-platform mobile app).

Backend:  Node.js + Express.js.

Database:  Firebase / MongoDB.

Integration:  APIs with prepaid meter systems and payment gateways.

 --------------------------------------------------How It Works--------------------------------------------------------------
 
Users connect the Smart Saver app to their prepaid meter.

The app reads usage data from the meter and displays it in real time.

Users receive alerts when units are running low.

They can top up instantly via the app using mobile banking or wallet.

Smart Saver analyzes usage patterns and gives insights for budgeting and saving.
